The Nuts and Bolts Pizza Recipe

What the heck is it?

Don't worry about it. It's not for you anyways. It's a robot thing. You wouldn't understand.

In case you were a robot though... Its literally nuts. Its literally bolts. Call over your spouse Alex or Alexa for an evening of Roflix and Chill.

Ingredients

Probably gave it away earlier but yeah, you guessed it. Its a bunch of nuts and bolts. Okay, okayyyy... there maaay beee a smaaaall chance of possibly a cheesey bit somewhere. But yeah, mostly just stuff that would otherwise go on a bicycle.

  • 100 nuts. Make sure they're not rusty either. They better be shiny and polished!
  • 100 bolts. These can be rusty. But not too much rust. You don't want to disappoint the robots. Don't ask.

Steps

  1. Take dough and sauce and oh... sorry, that's for humans. Okay let's try this again...

  2. Take a bicycle. Crush it with your robot arms.

  3. Remove the nuts and bolts.

  4. Put nuts and bolts onto a metallic plate and toss it in the oven. Actually don't because you might damage the oven.

  5. Serve with a drizzle of machine oil.
