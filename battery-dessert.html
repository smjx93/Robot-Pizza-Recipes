The Battery Dessert Pile

What the heck is it?

Interestingly enough a battery is called a "pile" in some languages becuase a long time ago they would pile up discs of stuff that stored electricity.

Well enough of the history lesson. Just ask your favorite robot, what he loves more than anything. The robot would probably say energy! Becuase whats a robot without electricity? Probably a useless machine? Well let's fix that with a little battery juice!

Ingredients

Literally go find a bunch of new batteries. It's really not that complicated.

Unless you don't know where to find them. Well in that case just ask your robot friend to help you before he runs out of electricity.

  • Piles of batteries. The more the merrier.
  • Some cables.
  • Whatever else you think makes a good battery.

Steps

  1. Put the batteries in neat row.

  2. Hook up all the cables to the robot.

  3. Flip the switch and watch your robot friend smile.

  4. Serve with a drizzle of machine oil. No, I'm kidding. No machine oil this time.
